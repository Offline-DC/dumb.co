Dumb Calendar

Your Google Calendar, on your Dumbphone.

Dumb Calendar is a built-in app on the Dumbphone from Offline DC. It shows your schedule at a glance — a today agenda, a month grid, a day view, and event details — designed for the phone's d-pad and buttons, no touchscreen required.

What it does

  • Connect your Google account once, right on the device, using Google's secure sign-in.
  • See your calendars and events on the Dumbphone — read-only. Dumb Calendar never creates, edits, or deletes anything in your Google Calendar.
  • Your schedule is stored on the device so it works offline, and your sign-in is kept in encrypted storage.
Your data, your control. Dumb Calendar only requests read-only calendar access, uses it solely to display your schedule on your device, and never sells it or uses it for ads. You can disconnect anytime from the app (settings → log out), which revokes access and deletes the cached data. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
